## How we build

- **Architecture first** — systems that stay understandable as they grow
- **Modular** — products share infrastructure where it makes sense
- **Quality > speed** — long-term maintainability matters more than launch dates
- **Real problems only** — no tech demo gimmicks, no AI for AI's sake

Our philosophy: software should integrate into how people already work. Make them faster, clearer, more in control — without adding friction.
